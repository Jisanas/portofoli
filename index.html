ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Jisana S, a frontend and backend developer passionate about building accessible, pixel-perfect web experiences. I specialize in creating intuitive and responsive user interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.

MY EXPERIENCE WITH STEYP

Steyp’s project-based learning and industry-focused curriculum stood out to me as a great opportunity to sharpen my skills. While I was initially interested in coding, I found myself eager to understand more about the program itself—its origin, how it evolved, and why it was designed in such a way. As I delved deeper, I realized how valuable it was to learn beyond just coding. The program helped me develop key skills in communication, presentation, and teamwork through group projects and delivering presentations to peers and mentors.

How Steyp Shaped Me
The program transformed me from a theoretical learner into a practical developer. Today, I approach challenges with resilience, equipped with a toolkit of modern technologies and the ability to deliver scalable solutions—thanks to Steyp’s rigorous training.

Highlight: One memorable experience was at Kadakkal Village Park, where a RoboXplorers event took place. I had the opportunity to lead a session on Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. This was my first offline class presentation, and it not only boosted my confidence but also enhanced my presentation skills. It was an invaluable experience, and I am grateful to Steyp and Kadakkal Village Park for providing this opportunity.
